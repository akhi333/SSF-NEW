Welcome to Starlink Security Force

Starlink Security Force Pvt. Ltd. is a professional security and facility services company founded in 2001 by Mr. Ashok, a veteran of the Indian Army and a Certified Security Practitioner. Built on discipline, integrity, and service excellence, the group has grown steadily by focusing on quality and long-term trust with associates, clients, and stakeholders. Today, the company delivers global‑standard security solutions across South India with a team of 5,000+ trained professionals.

Leadership

The company's foundation draws on Mr. Ashok's decades of defense experience and leadership in the service industry. His approach emphasizes mission readiness, rigorous training, and ethical conduct, ensuring every deployment upholds the brand promise of vigilance and reliability. A seasoned leadership team supports operations, compliance, and client success across all regions.

Presence

Headquarters are located in Cyberabad, Telangana, with a branch office in Kukatpally (KPHB Colony), enabling responsive support across the state and neighboring regions. Centralized command and regional field teams ensure rapid mobilization and 24x7 coordination. This footprint allows consistent service quality, localized supervision, and faster incident response.

Group Structure

Starlink Security Force Pvt. Ltd. operates as a wholly owned subsidiary of Action Group since 2012, combining the agility of a focused security provider with the governance and scalability of a larger enterprise. This affiliation strengthens procurement, technology adoption, and multi‑city deployment capabilities. Clients benefit from unified standards and single‑window program management.

What we do

Our services include manned guarding and physical security for corporate, industrial, residential, and public infrastructure. We provide executive protection and escort management with risk‑based routing and advanced planning. Additionally, we offer facility maintenance support integrated with security operations for continuity and efficiency.

People and training

Recruitment prioritizes background verification, physical fitness, and behavioral competencies suited to site requirements. Structured training covers access control, patrolling, incident reporting, emergency response, first aid, fire safety, and client SOPs. Periodic drills and refresher programs maintain readiness and improve on‑site performance metrics.

Operations and quality

Operations are driven by SOPs, site risk assessments, and measurable KPIs such as incident closure times, patrol compliance, and audit scores. Dedicated account managers and field officers conduct regular audits, safety walks, and stakeholder reviews. Continuous improvement loops align post orders, staffing, and technology to evolving risk profiles.

Technology enablement

Solutions are enhanced with digital guard tour systems, incident logging, and real‑time dashboards where applicable. Central monitoring supports escalations, analytics, and trend reporting to reduce recurrence and improve prevention. Technology choices remain pragmatic—selected to fit site risk, budget, and outcomes.

Industries served

We serve IT/ITES parks, corporate offices, and commercial real estate. Our expertise extends to manufacturing plants, logistics hubs, and warehouses. We also provide security for gated communities, educational campuses, healthcare facilities, and retail establishments.

Scale and reliability

With 5,000+ personnel and standardized deployment frameworks, the company can mobilize quickly for greenfield sites or scale existing contracts. Cross‑trained teams and relief strength protect service continuity during peak demand or emergencies. Vendor governance and strong supervision sustain consistent service levels.

Vision and values

The mission is to safeguard people, assets, and reputations through disciplined execution and respectful service. Core values—integrity, vigilance, teamwork, and accountability—guide every assignment. The guiding credo captures this spirit: "Eyes in the sky, shield on the ground."

Milestones

In 2001, the company was founded by Mr. Ashok with a focus on disciplined, defense‑grade security. In 2012, it became a wholly owned subsidiary of Action Group, accelerating expansion and systems maturity. From 2012 to present, we have expanded across South India with a growing portfolio of marquee clients and multi‑site programs.

Why Starlink

Clients choose Starlink for dependable manpower, transparent supervision, and measurable outcomes. The blend of military ethos, process rigor, and practical technology ensures sites are protected, compliant, and visitor‑friendly. Partnerships are built on responsiveness, respect, and results that stand the test of time.
